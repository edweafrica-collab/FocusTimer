Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

I found a bug or want to request a feature.

Open an issue on our GitHub repository. We read every issue and appreciate detailed bug reports.

Does FocusTimer work on Mac or Linux?

Currently, FocusTimer is built for Windows 10/11. Mac and Linux versions are on our roadmap. If you'd like to help port the app, see the collaboration section below!

Will FocusTimer slow down my computer?

No. FocusTimer is incredibly lightweight. It uses minimal CPU and memory, even when the timer is running. It's designed to stay out of your way.

Can I customize the warning colors?

Not yet, but it's on our feature list! Currently, the timer shows yellow at 5 minutes remaining and red when time is up. Custom themes are coming in a future release.

The viewer window isn't showing on my second monitor.

Make sure your second display is detected by Windows. FocusTimer will prompt you to move the viewer to a secondary screen if one is available. You can also manually drag the viewer window.

Can I use FocusTimer for free at my church/conference/school?

Absolutely! FocusTimer is free for personal, educational, and non-profit use. We built it specifically for churches, conferences, classrooms, and live streams.

I have a different question.

Send us an email at focustimer@example.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
